Aloo Tikki

Description:

Aloo Tikki is a popular Indian street food snack made from mashed potatoes, spices, and herbs. The mixture is shaped into small patties, shallow-fried until golden and crispy, and served with various chutneys and toppings.

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • Green peas, boiled and mashed
  • Bread crumbs
  • Green chilies, finely chopped
  • Ginger, grated
  • Coriander leaves, chopped
  • Garam masala
  • Red chili powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Oil, for shallow frying
