Dosa

Description:

Dosa is a popular South Indian crepe made from fermented rice and urad dal (black gram) batter. It is a versatile dish, often enjoyed with a variety of chutneys and sambar. Dosas can be plain or stuffed with various fillings, making them a delicious and satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Urad dal (black gram)
  • Fenugreek seeds
  • Salt, to taste
  • Oil or ghee, for cooking
  • Optional fillings (e.g., potato masala, paneer, or vegetables)
  • Chutneys and sambar for serving
